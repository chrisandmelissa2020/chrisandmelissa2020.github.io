Transportation

For those who are flying, we recommend flying into Seattle-Tacoma International Airport (SEA-TAC).

For those staying in Seattle, we will be providing a shuttle to the wedding (details here).

While we recommend taking the shuttle, those who are staying closer to the wedding venue and have rented a car can also drive. Driving instructions here.

Where to stay

We've reserved a couple of hotel blocks in Seattle at a discounted rate! As we will be running a shuttle between Seattle and the venue, we recommend staying somewhere within Seattle, as there are many more things to see, eat, and explore!

For those who would like to rent a car and stay closer to the wedding venue, there are additional options available (although we have not booked a hotel block:

  • Camino Island
  • AirBnB
